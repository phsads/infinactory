NOTES:
If there are no * next to circuits (input, machines), that means that better circuits of the same tier can be used.

I. Tier 0
  1. Machines
    - Manual Press: turns ingots to plates (max: 6; cost: free)
    - Manual Cutter: turns plates to wires (max: 6; cost: free)
    - Coal Furnace: turns ores into ingots at an inefficient rate (max: 6; cost: free)
    - Assembler: assembles stuff (max: 8; cost: free)
    - Basic Research Lab: consumes items to make science packs (max: 1; cost: free)
    - Buffer: holds 20 items of the same type (max: 8; cost: free)
  2. Recipes
    - 1 ore -> (Coal Furnace) 1 ingot
    - 1 ingot -> (Manual Press) 1 plate
    - 1 plate -> (Manual Press) 12 foil
    - 1 coal -> (Manual Press): 1 coal dust
    - 1 plate -> (Manual Cutter) 4 wire
    - 1 plank -> (Manual Cutter) 1 wood tube
    - 1 wood -> (Manual Cutter) 8 plank
    - 3 copper foil + 4 wood plank -> (Assembler) 1 primitive circuit board
    - 6 copper wire + 2 coal dust + 3 plank tube -> (Assembler) 3 primitive resistors
    - 2 copper wire + 6 tin foil + 1 plank tube -> (Assembler) 1 primitive diode
    - 1 primitive circuit board + 8 primitive resistors + 3 primitive diodes -> (Assembler) 1 primitive circuit
    - 1 primitive circuit* -> (Research Center) 8 red science pack
    - 1 red science pack + 6 iron plate -> (Assembler) 1 basic machine casing
    
II. Tier 1
  1. Machines
    - Steam Engine: Uses water and coal to generate 8W (max: unlimited; cost: 1 basic MC, 6 copper plate, 4 copper rotor, 4 tin wire)
    - Basic Cutter: Electric powered cutter (max: unlimited; cost: 1 basic MC, 24 copper foil, 4 tin wire, 2 primitive circuit)
    - Basic Press: Electric powered presser (max: unlimited; cost: 1 basic MC, 3 iron plate, 4 tin wire, 2 primitive circuit)
    - Basic Assembler: Electric powered assembler (max: unlimited; cost: 1 basic MC, 4 tin wire, 12 primitive circuit)
    - Tier 1 cable: Transfer 32W per connection (max: unlimited; cost: 8 tin wire)
    - Basic Electric Furnace: Electric powered furnace (max: unlimited; cost: 1 basic MC, 32 copper wire, 4 tin wire, 2 primitive circuit)
    - Basic Alloy Smelter: Creates alloys (max: unlimited; cost: 1 basic MC, 48 copper wire, 4 tin wire, 3 primitive circuit)
    - Multiblock Controller: Used for multiblocks (max: unlimited; cost: 1 basic MC, 2 primitive multiprocessor)
    - Multiblock Casing: Used for multiblocks (max: unlimited; cost: 1 basic MC)
    - Basic Resource Miner: Harvests resources (max: unlimited; cost: 1 basic MC, 8 iron plate, 4 tin wire, 8 primtive circuit)
    - Basic Terrain Miner: Harvests resources from terrain (max: unlimited; cost: 1 basic MC, 8 tin plate, 4 tin wire, 8 primitive circuit)
    - Advanced Research Lab: Electric research lab (max: unlimited; cost: 8 basic MC, 32 basic circuit, 16 tin wire, 12 copper plate)
    - Basic Elemental Reducer: Splits items into elements (max: ulm; cost: 1 basic MC, 4 copper wire, 4 tin wire, 2 primitive circuit)
    - Fluid Input Hatch I: 10 units (max: ulm; cost: 1 basic MC)
    - Item Input Hatch I: 50 items (max: ulm; cost: 1 basic MC)
    - Fluid Output Hatch I: 10 units (max: ulm; cost: 1 basic MC)
    - Item Output Hatch I: 50 items (max: ulm; cost: 1 basic MC)
    - Energy Input Hatch I: holds 256J, accepts 32W (max: ulm; cost: 1 basic MC)
    - Energy Output Hatch I: holds 256J, pushes 32W (max: ulm; cost: 1 basic MC)
    - Item Conveyor Point: for item transfer (4 items / s) (max: ulm; cost: 1 primitive circuit, 1 basic MC)
    - Basic Item Filter: allows insertion of 3 selected items (cost: 1 basic MC, 2 primitive circuit)
    - Basic Fluid Filter: allows insertion of 3 selected fluids (cost: 1 basic MC, 2 primitive circuit)
  2. Recipes
    - 2 copper ingot + 1 tin ingot -> (Alloy Smelter) 3 bronze ingot
    - 1 gold ingot + 1 silver ingot -> (Alloy Smelter) 2 electrum ingot
    - 1 oil -> (oil processor) 3 plastic sheets
    - 4 oil -> (oil processor) 3 fuel
    - 1 sand -> (furnace) 1 glass
    - 17 clay -> (elemental reducer) 2Si + 2Al + 4H2 + 9O2
    - 1 primitive circuit board + 2 plastic + 8 gold foil -> 1 plastic circuit board
    - 8 primitive circuit + 2 glass plane + 6 gold wire -> 1 primitive multiprocessor
